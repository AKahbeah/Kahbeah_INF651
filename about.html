About Budget Buddy

Budget Buddy is your personal expense tracker, designed to help you manage your money wisely with real-time interactivity and simple visual breakdowns.

It was created as a student project for INF 651 to demonstrate JavaScript concepts like DOM manipulation, arrays, loops, and conditional logic.

  • Track your daily spending
  • Organize by category
  • Filter and visualize totals
